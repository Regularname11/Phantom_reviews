Just Dance
Just Dance is a motion-based dancing game for multiple players, with each game including a collection of classic and modern songs each with their own dance choreographies. During each song, players mirror a dance performed by actors on the screen, following commands that appear on the screen, and are awarded for their accuracy. Additionally, there are gold moves in which players must strike a pose in order to earn bonus points.
